# Generating safe and transparent data with metasyn


What if want to share a dataset for analysis or collaboration, the data is sensitive, containing personal information, confidential business records, or details that could be misused? 

Synthetic data generation can help here, by allowing you to create a dataset that captures the essence of your original data – its structure, variable types, and basic relationships – without revealing any confidential details. However, the problem with that is that it generally is not is transparent how the data was created. Therefore, metasyn goes one step beyond that, and allows you to share not just the synthetic data, but also the data generation model as an easy to read and modify text file.

This allows collaborators to examine and understand how the synthetic data is generated. By sharing the data generation model alongside the synthetic data, you also allow others to reproduce your results and even generate their own similar synthetic datasets for further code testing.
